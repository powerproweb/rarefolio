One bar = one serial number. Every CNFT linked to a specific bar shares the same Bar Serial # for that bar’s full run.

For Bar I , the Bar Serial # is: E101837. This value does not change across the entire 40,000 CNFTs associated with Bar I.

Each CNFT still has its own unique ID. CNFTs are labeled individually using IDs like qd-silver-0000001, then qd-silver-0000002, and so on, counting upward for each new CNFT.

In practice: the serial identifies the physical bar, while the qd-silver ID identifies the individual CNFT within that bar’s full series.
