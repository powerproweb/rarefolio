What Your Download Package Includes

Your download package includes RGB JPG files designed for fast loading, sharp screen presentation, and clean digital use. These are ideal for online viewing, personal display, collection previews, social sharing, and modern screen-based environments.

Your package also includes CMYK PNG files prepared for more accurate print reproduction. These files are intended for collectors who want to create physical prints for walls, offices, studios, gallery-style presentations, or private collection displays.

  • 4000 x 4000 px at 300 DPI
    Best for premium wall prints, office presentation, gallery-style display, and private collector printing.

What You Are Not Receiving

To keep everything clear and honest, here is what your Rarefolio download package does not include by default:

  • No physical silver redemption.
    Rarefolio CNFTs may use a silver bar mindset, serial structure, or Silver Bar Token context, but they are not redeemable for physical silver, metal, bullion, coins, bars, or any physical commodity.
  • No claim to physical precious metal.
    The silver-bar concept provides narrative, provenance, and collector context. It does not create a right, claim, entitlement, or ownership interest in physical silver.
  • No automatic commercial printing service.
    You receive production-ready digital files, but physical printing, framing, shipping, installation, and local print-shop coordination are not included unless separately arranged.
  • No custom resizing by default.
    Standard package sizes are included. Larger files and specialized formats are available as upgrade services.
  • No guarantee of color match across every printer or screen.
    Screens, print shops, paper types, inks, and lighting conditions vary. For serious print work, use a professional printer and request a proof.

The clean version: you are collecting premium digital artwork with provenance-first structure, not buying a silver redemption certificate. Beautiful, powerful, honest. That is the Rarefolio lane.

Recommended Download Care

Once you receive your files, we recommend saving them in more than one place. Keep one copy on your main computer, one copy on an external drive, and one backup in secure cloud storage. Treat your files like collector assets, because that is exactly how they are intended to function.

  • Do not rename master files unless you keep the original file names backed up.
  • Do not compress the print files before sending them to a print provider.
  • Use the RGB JPG files for screens and digital display.
  • Use the CMYK PNG files for print conversations and print preparation.
  • Request a print proof before committing to expensive large-format printing.
