2) Information We Collect

A) Information you provide to us

We may collect information you choose to submit, such as:

  • Contact details (name, email) if you subscribe, contact support, or join a waitlist
  • Messages and form submissions (including any information you include)
  • Optional preferences (if enabled)

B) Information collected automatically

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system, language, referring URLs, pages viewed, clicks, and timestamps

We may use cookies/local storage to keep the site functional (for example, remembering UI settings), understand traffic and performance, and reduce fraud and abuse.

D) Blockchain/public ledger information (important)

If you interact with CNFTs via third-party wallets, marketplaces, or blockchains:

  • Public blockchain data is public by design (e.g., wallet addresses, transaction hashes, token IDs, timestamps).
  • We do not control these networks and generally cannot delete or modify on-chain data.

3) How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service (render pages, load collections/batches, improve performance)

  • Send updates if you opt in (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms

4) How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information in the ordinary commercial sense.

We may share information in these cases:

  • Legal and safety: to comply with law, court orders, or protect rights, safety, and integrity of the Service
  • Business changes: merger, acquisition, financing, or asset transfer (with appropriate safeguards)
  • With your direction: if you explicitly ask us to share something

5) Analytics and Advertising

We may use analytics tools to understand traffic and performance. These tools may collect device/usage data and set cookies.

If you later add advertising, retargeting, or marketing pixels, you should update this policy and provide opt-out controls where required.

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Service and meet legitimate business needs (security, auditing, dispute resolution), or comply with legal obligations.
