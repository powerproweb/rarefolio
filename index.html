The Thesis

Why Rarefolio Exists

Most NFT collections chase volume. Rarefolio chases permanence. Every CNFT is mapped to a specific 100 ounce .999 silver bar by serial number, presented at museum quality, and issued in limited blocks built for long-horizon collectors — not ephemeral traders.

Tokenized silver bar provenance. Collector-grade presentation. Cardano infrastructure. Limited release architecture. An archive built to outlast the cycle.

Trust & Standards

How the Vault is Governed

The infrastructure of a serious collection — made explicit.

Serial Logic

Each CNFT inherits its parent bar's physical serial (e.g. E101837) and carries a deterministic ID of the form qd-silver-XXXXXXX.

Release Structure

40,000 CNFTs per bar, issued in named blocks. No surprise inflation. No silent mints. Schedule is published and honored.

Collection Integrity

Curated series only. Each piece belongs to a titled exhibition, not an anonymous generative batch.

Secondary Market Vision

Rarefolio is built for holding first and trading second. A dedicated marketplace is on the roadmap for verified Rarefolio provenance.
